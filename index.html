Our Mission

The mission of The Meet's Book Bank is to provide high quality resources and services to support learning and teaching at The Meet's. To serve our academic community, the Book Bank teaches students to seek, evaluate, and use information for college success and lifelong learning.

Our Vision

The vision of The Meet's Book Bank is to be a vibrant environment where students are encouraged to research, learn, complete coursework, collaborate, and study.The Meet's Book Bank wants all students, faculty, staff, and community members to become information literate knowledge seekers who will be able to find, evaluate, and utilize resources in a variety of formats and embrace the Book Bank as a point of connection to information.

Prohibition's in Book Bank

The book bank is designed to provide students with a quiet place for reading, research and study.
  • Food is not permitted in the Book Bank
  • Drinks with closeable lids are permitted
  • Smoking is not permitted in the Book Bank and is illegal by state law
  • Cell Phones are not permitted in the Book Bank
The book bank Staff maintains a friendly and approachable demeanor at all times, but will not hesitate to contact campus police if excessively loud and disruptive behavior persists in the Book Bank.
